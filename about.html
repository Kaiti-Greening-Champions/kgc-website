Our Mission

Our aim is to train the community at the grassroots level to embrace and champion a culture that promotes environmental conservation and improvement. This will be achieved through an ecosystem that propagates sustainable micro-agribusinesses, providing a means to sustainably improve livelihoods.

Our Team

Prof. Emmanuel Mutisya

Patron

Prof. Mutisya is a long-term consultant and programme coordinator in the education and skills development sector at the African Development Bank. His expertise spans international development policy, project management, the design of skills development programmes, policy strategic planning, youth entrepreneurship, financial inclusion, higher education and research, and sustainability science. Prof. Mutisya has a track record of designing, formulating, implementing, and monitoring portfolios at the country, regional, and global levels. Currently, he serves as University Council Chairman, Kenya-AIST, Education Advisor to the President, and Chairman of GESCI.

PS. Teresia Mbaika

Trustee

Teresia Mbaika, the current Permanent Secretary of the State Department of Devolution, is an environmentalist and health economist with over 10 years’ experience in the public sector. She has been at the forefront of the greening agenda as an active Kaiti Greening Champions member.

Serah M. Kisilu

Trustee

Serah Kisilu is passionate about greening and water conservation. She holds a B.Sc. in environmental health, a diploma in pharmaceutical technology, and a certificate in pharmaceutical supply chain management. Currently, in the pharmaceutical industry (Semular Pharmacy).

Dr. Muthenya Muia

Trustee

Dr Muthenya is a founding member of the Kaiti Greening Champions and a passionate greener. He is also a consultant general/hepatopancreatobiliary surgeon.

Douglas Kasimu

Chairperson

Douglas Nguma Kasimu is the current chairman of Kaiti Greening Champions. He is also the ceo of Sethstar Enterprises Limited, a green renewable energy company based in Nairobi specialising in the solarization of boreholes, dairy farms, homes, and value-added chains in need of alternative power from solar across Kenya. He is also a member of the governing council at the Kenya Cultural Centre, serving as a non-executive director since February 2023. Douglas Nguma holds a diploma in electrical engineering with over 6 years of experience in solar energy production across Kenya.

Mbindyo Kisilu Mulwa

Vice Chairperson

Founder member since 2016. A committed environmentalist committed to the greening agenda. He is very passionate about sensitising the community about greening the environment to mitigate climate change risks. He has been involved in planting many trees in his homestead and in groups in schools across Kaiti and Makueni

CPA. Alfred Mwau

Treasurer

Alfred is a CPAK finalist with over 15 years’ experience in Kenya and Tanzania. He has extensive experience in manufacturing, internal audit, FMCG, Marketing and advertising.

Francis Muendo

Secretary

Francis Muendo is an insurance professional with eight years of experience in the insurance industry. He is the CEO of Greatscope Insurance Agency, an insurance consultancy and brokerage firm based in Nairobi. He also serves at the Council of Insurance Institute of Kenya as an Executive Council Member. Francis is a passionate environmentalist with great interest in farming in rural Makueni, where he runs Southern Sun Farm, a venture that specialises in livestock, fruit and vegetable farming.

Aurelia Mumbua

Assistant Secretary

Aurelia is a Communication Professional with a vast experience in the humanitarian sector. She is a a passionate small farmer, greener and environmentalist. She is a strong believer in mentoring the younger generation, an education enthusiast and a promoter for equity in education.

MCIPS. Caleb Mbithi

Organizing Secretary

Caleb Mbithi, MCIPS, is a supply chain management expert with experience spanning over 8 years in the humanitarian sector, an entrepreneur, teacher, digital strategist, author, farmer, and passionate greener planet advocate. Caleb holds a bachelors degree in supply chain management from JKUAT, an MBA (operations management) from the University of Nairobi, and a CIPS Professional Diploma level 6 from the Chartered Institute of Procurement and Supply in the UK, among many other professional certifications. He serves on several management and educational boards. He is a founder and the current organising secretary for Kaiti Greening Champions.

Ruth Mboya

Communications Lead

Ruth is an advocate of sustainable development goals focusing on climate action, quality education, and gender equality. Outside of her passion for advocacy work, she is a public relations and development communications expert with over 7 years’ experience in communications and marketing. She is also a trainer and a consultant in the field of communications.

Kaiti Greening Champions
