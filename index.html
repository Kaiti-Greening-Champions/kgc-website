Our Vision is ....

To Passionately Conserve our Environment by actively being involved in all greening activities and practices (e.g. maximum utilization of Green Energy, tree nursery set-ups, tree planting, climate change trainings) and in overall implementation of the 3R’s “Mottainai koto” (Reduce, Reuse, Recycle) Principle.

Download Brochure

Our Mission is ....

To enhance the county’s forest by reforestation as a climate change mitigation strategy, reduce poverty by providing alternative livelihood activities for marginalized upland and lowland households in relation to seedling production and care and maintenance of newly-planted trees and other greening projects that shall be initiated for an overall achievement of our Vision.

To meet like-minded people

Meet other people who are passionate about environmental conservation. You can make new friends, share ideas, and learn from each other. This can be a great way to stay motivated and inspired to make a difference.

To make a difference

KGC works on the frontlines of environmental conservation, and we need your help to make a real impact. By joining us, you can be part of a team of dedicated individuals who are working to protect our planet.

To learn new skills

We offer a great way to learn new skills and gain experience in environmental conservation. With training and development opportunities to our members, so you can learn new skills and knowledge that you can use in your career or in your personal life.
