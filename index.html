Our Vision is ....

Our goal is to nurture a generation that is not only concerned about the environment but also committed to living in a socially and economically sustainable way to foster a green environment.

Download Brochure

Our Mission is ....

Our aim is to train the community at the grassroots level to embrace and champion a culture that promotes environmental conservation and improvement. This will be achieved through an ecosystem that propagates sustainable micro-agribusinesses, providing a means to sustainably improve livelihoods.

To meet like-minded people

Meet other people who are passionate about environmental conservation. You can make new friends, share ideas, and learn from each other. This can be a great way to stay motivated and inspired to make a difference.

To make a difference

KGC works on the frontlines of environmental conservation, and we need your help to make a real impact. By joining us, you can be part of a team of dedicated individuals who are working to protect our planet.

To learn new skills

We offer a great way to learn new skills and gain experience in environmental conservation. With training and development opportunities to our members, so you can learn new skills and knowledge that you can use in your career or in your personal life.
